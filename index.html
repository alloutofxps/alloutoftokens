About

The
person
behind it.

I grew up in finance. Started as an articled assistant in Bengaluru, worked my way through Big-4 audits across sectors, moved countries, and somewhere along the way started noticing that the job I was doing was changing faster than most people in the room were willing to admit.

The AI piece isn't a side interest. I've been building it into my actual work — using it on real engagements, pitching it to partners, joining innovation teams. I think the finance professionals who figure out how to work alongside these tools — rather than pretend they don't exist — are going to be the ones who matter in 10 years.

Outside of work: motorcycles, basketball, dramas, and collecting other people's stories like they're alternate versions of my own. Based in Almere, the Netherlands. ACCA (9/13 papers), CA Finalist, B.Com (8.2 CGPA).
